Accommodation

Madanapalle Institute of Technology & Science (MITS) is offering accommodation for students from other colleges participating in ASHV 2K26 events.

The accommodation is available at a daily rate of Rs 200/-(Free accommodation is provided for sports persons), allowing participants to stay on campus throughout the event duration.

This initiative is particularly beneficial for students traveling long distances as it eliminates the need for separate lodging arrangements.

The accommodation opportunity fosters a sense of camaraderie among participants from different colleges.

It facilitates closer interaction and collaboration during their stay, enhancing the overall experience.

With accommodation worries addressed, participants can fully immerse themselves in the events.

The accommodation likely includes essential amenities for a comfortable stay, ensuring attendees' needs are met.

Due to anticipated demand, advance booking is advisable to secure accommodations.

This initiative reflects MITS's commitment to supporting ASHV 2K26 events and enhancing the experience for all participants.

It underscores the institute's dedication to fostering a conducive environment for academic and extracurricular activities.
